Italian Hot Dog

Description

A recipe for an NJ-style Italian Hot Dog.

Ingredients

  • Italian roll / pizza bread
  • 1 to 2 green peppers, sliced
  • 1 sliced onion
  • potatoes cut into wedges or chunks
  • hot dogs, natural casing if possible
  • ketchup and/or mustard
  • salt

Steps

  1. Fry the potatoes in oil, adding salt once browned
  2. Remove potatoes and brown the peppers and onions, salting when done
  3. Fry hot dogs in oil to taste
  4. Add mustard to inside of roll if desired. Place hot dog in roll and and add vegetables until roll is full.
  5. Top with ketchup if desired and enjoy.
