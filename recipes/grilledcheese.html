Grilled Cheese

Description

This is a recipe to make a grilled cheese sandwich.

Ingredients

  • Sliced White Bread
  • Cheddar Cheese
  • Butter

Steps

  1. Preheat pan
  2. Butter both slices of bread. Place one butter side down in skillet and add cheese on top.
  3. Place other slice of bread butter side up on top
  4. Cook until browned, then flip and repeat until done.
