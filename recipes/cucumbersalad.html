Cucumber Dill Salad

Description

This is a recipe for a Polish-style cucumber dill salad. Delicious any time but especially in summer.

Ingredients

  • Cucumbers
  • Sour Cream
  • Lemon Juice
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Chopped Fresh Dill

Steps

  1. Mix sour cream with lemon juice and dill
  2. Slice cucumbers and add to sour cream mix
  3. Add salt and pepper and stir together
  4. Optionally, chill for 1 hour before serving
